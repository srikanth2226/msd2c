Range Rover, a prestigious model line from British automaker Land Rover, is celebrated for its luxury, off-road capability, and advanced technology. Launched in 1970, it combines rugged performance with refined comfort, making it a favorite for both urban and adventurous driving. The Range Rover lineup includes various models, from the standard Range Rover to the sportier Range Rover Sport and the more compact Range Rover Velar. It features a blend of sophisticated design, high-end materials, and state-of-the-art infotainment and driver-assistance systems. Its strong reputation is built on exceptional off-road prowess and luxurious interior appointments.
